Getting Started Guide

Signing up to the Kilkenny Cats platform is easy.


For Mobile:

To access the platform on your mobile device, download the MyClub 360 mobile app.

  1. Select “Kilkenny Cats” as your Club.
  2. Hit Register and Enter your Details.
  3. Start Exploring the Kilkenny Cats Platform!

For Web:

Visit the Kilkenny Cats site by clicking on the link: Kilkenny Cats Registration

  1. Fill in the registration form to create your account and select SUBMIT.
  2. You will be taken directly to the Homepage of the Kilkenny Cats platform. You can now start exploring the platform.

If you need any guidance or support, please contact the support team at support@myclubireland.com
