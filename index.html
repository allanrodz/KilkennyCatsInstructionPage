Getting Started Guide

Signing up to your Kilkenny Cats platform is easy.


Follow these steps:

For Web:

Visit the Kilkenny Cats site by clicking on the link below: Kilkenny Cats Registration

  1. Select the Register button.
  2. This will bring you to the registration page. Fill in the registration form to create your account and select SUBMIT.
  3. You will be taken directly to the Homepage of the Kilkenny Cats platform. You can now start exploring the platform.

For Mobile:

To access the platform on your mobile device, download the MyClub 360 mobile app.

  1. Download the MyClub360 App.
  2. Select “Kilkenny Cats” as your Club.
  3. Hit Register and Enter your Details (Make sure to hit “Done” after Selecting Gender and DOB).
  4. Start Exploring the Kilkenny Cats Platform!

If you need any guidance or support, please contact the support team at support@myclubireland.com
